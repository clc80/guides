## Logical Operators

* **And** (&&)
* **Or** (||)
* **Not** (!)
* **If**
* **Iff** (if and only if)
* **Xor** ("eXclusive or")

* **true AND true** = true
* **true OR true** = true
* **true AND false** = false
* **true OR false** = true
* **false AND false** = false
* **false OR false** = false
